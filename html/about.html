About Me

Hi! I'm a designer and artist, originally from Northern Ontario! Thunder Bay, to be specific. You can call me M. I've been creating for just about as long as I can remember, everything from acrylic paintings, to ceramics, to digital design. I love working with lots of different colours and styles, and I take inspiration for my designs from comics, cartoons, and the natural world. Take a look at some of my work, and if you find yourself liking what I've got, lets chat!
